Overview of English Word Usage

This reference page summarizes general observations about the size, structure, and usage frequency of words in the English language. Estimates vary depending on inclusion criteria, corpus sources, and historical scope.

Estimated total English lexical items: approximately 1,025,000

Word frequency is highly uneven. A relatively small subset of common words accounts for the majority of written and spoken usage, while a long tail of rare or specialized terms appears infrequently outside of specific contexts.
